reimagine Your Life

Reimagine your life at The Alexander – where home meets everything you love about your favorite high-end resort. Located in the heart of Central Oregon, The Alexander redefines active living for those 55 and better. Offering stunning views of the Cascade Range and high desert landscapes, you’ll find an array of unique floorplan options along with comfortable living and a focus on holistic wellness that delivers the distinguished, adventurous, fun and carefree lifestyle you’ve always imagined.

WHERE PINOT AND PADDLEBOARDS MEET

Thanks to The Alexander’s location in Central Oregon, living an active, engaged lifestyle comes with the territory. Ride bicycles on city paths or rugged trails. Enjoy world-class golf with a stunning selection of public and private courses. Take a morning hike to Tumalo Falls. Paddleboard Hosmer Lake in the afternoon, then dine on exceptional Northwest cuisine in downtown Bend that evening. Ski the slopes of Mt. Bachelor or go fly fishing on the Deschutes River. Not to mention the art, music, theater, shows, festivals, museums and other cultural events available every month of the year in beautiful Bend and throughout Central Oregon. When you choose to live at The Alexander, the fun comes naturally.
